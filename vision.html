Mission & Vision

Mission

Berkshire Hathaway's mission, led by Warren E. Buffett, is to maximize long-term shareholder value through the acquisition and management of strong, ethical, and undervalued businesses.

Vision

Its vision focuses on stability, trust, and investing in businesses with durable competitive advantages.
